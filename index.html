Where I Started

My first language I chose to learn was Python. I had never programmed before and after college finals I decided to spend my Christmas break learning Python. I loved it!! I tried to learn it from many angles. I used codeacademy to begin learning the basic syntax and basic concepts. I continued to look for books and other online courses to continue learning. I would also give myself my own projects to challenge myself. I began to use Python for data analysis and loved it.
